Midnight Lizard
Web-Extension Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is Midnight Lizard's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information being processed by the extension downloaded from one of these addresses:

  1. Chrome Web Store:
    https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/midnight-lizard/pbnndmlekkboofhnbonilimejonapojg

  2. Firefox Add-ons:
    https://addons.mozilla.org/firefox/addon/midnight-lizard-quantum

If you have downloaded this extension from any other location this privacy policy would not be applicable to it since its source code might have been altered.

Midnight Lizard web-extension does not collect any of your personal information, browsing history, IP addresses, etc. Also it does not sell or transfer your personal information to advertisers or other third-parties.

Midnight Lizard web-extension does not use browser cookies. To optimize initial page load it stores last applied brightness level in localStorage. To optimize consequent page loads it caches styles and images processing results in sessionStorage.

To store user settings Midnight Lizard web-extension uses chrome.storage in Chromium-based browsers and browser.storage in Firefox. By default user settings are synchronized between user devices but this behavior can be opted out on the Global settings tab.

Midnight Lizard web-extension has links to external sites that are not operated by us. Please be aware that we have no control over the content and practices of these sites, and cannot accept responsibility or liability for their respective privacy policies.

In the future Midnight Lizard web-extension might include user login form in order to provide some personalized services to the user. Any personal information exchange related to this will be a subject of the Website Privacy Policy.

This policy is effective as of 1 January 2019.
